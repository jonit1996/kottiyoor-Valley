Discover Kottiyoor

Beautiful destinations just minutes from your room.

Kottiyoor Valley Park & Resort serves as the perfect basecamp for exploring the rich heritage and lush wildlife of the Kannur district. Here is what is waiting for you nearby.

Kottiyoor Mahadeva Temple

10 Mins Away Pilgrimage

Known as the "Dakshina Kasi" of the South, this ancient temple is nestled deep in the forest. It hosts the famous annual Vaisakha Mahotsavam festival and offers a deeply spiritual atmosphere surrounded by the Bavali river.

Ask for Cab Info

Palchuram Waterfalls

25 Mins Away Nature & Trekking

A spectacular multi-tiered waterfall located near the Wayanad border. The trek down to the falls takes you through dense, pristine forests, rewarding you with icy cool mountain water and incredible photo opportunities.

Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary

40 Mins Away Wildlife Safari

Spread over the western slopes of the Western Ghats, this sanctuary is home to elephants, leopards, and diverse bird species. It is a fantastic day trip for nature enthusiasts and photographers staying at our resort.
