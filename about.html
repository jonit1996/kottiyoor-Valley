The Beginning

A Peaceful Retreat in Nature

Located in the serene surroundings of Chungakkunnu, Kottiyoor, our resort was built with a clear vision: to create a space where families, friends, and nature lovers can disconnect from the busy city life and reconnect with the outdoors.

We are not a concrete jungle; we are a valley park. We pride ourselves on offering clean, comfortable, and practical accommodations that blend perfectly with the lush green landscapes of Kerala.
